Types of Animals

Mammals

Mammals are warm-blooded vertebrates characterized by the presence of mammary glands.

Birds

Birds are feathered, winged, two-legged, warm-blooded, egg-laying vertebrates.

Fish

Fish are aquatic vertebrates that breathe through gills and have fins for swimming.

Reptiles

Reptiles are cold-blooded vertebrates covered in scales and lay eggs on land.

Amphibians

Amphibians are cold-blooded vertebrates with moist skin and undergo metamorphosis.

Invertebrates

Invertebrates are animals without a backbone, including insects, spiders, and worms.
